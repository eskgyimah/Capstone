Research Challenge

The COVID-19 pandemic generated unprecedented volumes of scientific literature across multiple databases. Researchers need efficient, automated tools to:

  • Collect data from multiple academic APIs

  • Generate comprehensive analysis reports

  • Create configurable quality scoring models
  • Generate publication-ready reports
